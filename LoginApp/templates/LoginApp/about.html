Speech Emotion Recognition

Overview:

Speech Emotion Recognition is a topic which applies the Concept of Machine Learning.
In simple terms, the model takes in the audio input and gives the output as the emotion behind the input audio/speech like 'Angry', 'Sad', 'Happy', etc.

Introduction:

In the era of machine learning & AI, we are trying to make a number of advancements in technology & make machines more like humans. Emotion plays an extremely important role in human mental life. It is a medium of expression of one’s perspective of one’s perspective or one’s mental state to others. Implementing the concept of Speech Emotion Recognition in machine learning is certainly essential to move forward in the pathway to AI. A system that recognises human emotion can act accordingly and provide a user friendly input. In future, a self functioning robot that recognises emotions through speech can increase the range of their spectrum.

Miscellaneous Info:

  • Dataset Used:
    • Ryerson Audio-Visual Database of Emotional Speech and Song (RAVDESS)
    • Toronto emotional speech set (TESS)

  • Models Tested:
    • Convolutional Neural Network(CNN)
    • MultiLayer Perceptron(MLP)
